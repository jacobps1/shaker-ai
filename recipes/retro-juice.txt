Retro Juice Cocktail Recipe

Ingredients (per drink):
- 1.5 oz Raspberry Vodka
- 0.75 oz Peach Schnapps
- 0.5 oz Triple Sec
- 2 oz Sour Mix
- Splash (1–2 oz) of Sprite

Instructions:
1. Fill a shaker with ice.
2. Add raspberry vodka, peach schnapps, triple sec, and sour mix.
3. Shake well for 10–15 seconds.
4. Strain into an ice-filled glass.
5. Top with a splash of Sprite.
6. Garnish with a cherry or lemon wedge (optional).
