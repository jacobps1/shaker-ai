Dragon Punch Cocktail Recipe

Ingredients (per drink):
- 1 oz Light Rum
- 1 oz Coconut Rum
- 0.5 oz Blue Curaçao
- 2 oz Sour Mix
- 2 oz Pineapple Juice

Instructions:
1. Fill a shaker with ice.
2. Add light rum, coconut rum, blue curaçao, sour mix, and pineapple juice.
3. Shake well for 10–15 seconds.
4. Strain into an ice-filled glass.
5. Garnish with a pineapple wedge or cherry (optional).
